Our Ongoimg Projects

Geospatial Analysis of Mortality Trends in Nandi County


Through the lens of Geographic Information Systems (GIS), we are conducting a comprehensive analysis to understand the spatial distribution and trends of deaths within the region. By harnessing the power of geospatial data, our project aims to provide valuable insights for informed decision-making on the causes of mortality. Stay tuned for updates as we delve deeper into this vital research endeavor.

Mapping Bribery spots on Kenyan Roads


Our latest initiative focuses on shedding light on the situation of bribery along Kenyan roads, particularly concerning interactions between motorists and traffic officers. Through crowdsourced data collection using ODK Collect, individuals like you have the power to contribute firsthand observations, pinpointing locations where these unethical practices occur.

Land Subsidence Characterization using GNSS and InSAR techniques in Nakuru County


Land subsidence has been a major issue in Nakuru County in recent years. The region of Nakuru County, which is well-known for its high population density and urbanization, has undergone ground subsidence as a result of a number of reasons, including both natural and man-made ones.
